Polygon Project





Imagine a canvas where shapes come alive—this is the Polygon Project. It turns basic programming into a colorful playground, blending object-oriented design with a dash of fun experimentation.

Two main classes—Vertex and Polygon—bring order to creative chaos. Vertices store points, polygons assemble them into dynamic, eye-catching shapes, each with vibrant colors and playful transparency.

With a few clicks, you can build shapes from scratch. Left-click to place vertices, right-click to complete your polygon, and watch as your geometric creations spring to life on the screen.

The application also supports intuitive keyboard controls: press e to erase the most recent polygon, c to clear the canvas, or use W, A, S, D to move selected shapes around. This makes interacting with your polygons not just functional, but downright fun!

Behind the scenes, the project demonstrates key programming concepts like encapsulation, modular design, and event handling. Each polygon is a small masterpiece of code, proving that even simple structures can produce complex, visually compelling outcomes.

Overall, the Polygon Project is more than just code—it’s an experiment in creativity, precision, and interactive design. It invites users to play, explore, and discover how programming principles transform into colorful, dynamic geometric art.
